High Society

I think I mentioned already that I’ve been rereading Cerebus comics. The first time I read them, I never did get my hands on ”High Society” or ”Church and State”; I had read the first volume out of a library, and then started with “Jaka’s Story” through “Melmoth” and most of the “Mothers & Daughters” story line. Then I fell off the bus for a bit. I’ve now caught up to about issue 226 (part way through “Rick’s Story”). So, I’m well past “re-reading” and have moved on to just “reading”. Looking forward to the conclusion (the entire run of Cerebus is 300 issues, an amazing accomplishment by a single creative team).

Sim’s a fantastic artist, as is his co-conspirator Gerhard, and a great story-teller, both visually and simply in prose (there’s a lot of straight prose in certain parts of Cerebus). Well worth a read.
